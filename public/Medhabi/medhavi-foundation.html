Medhavi Foundation is a Non-Profit company under section 25 that was incorporated in the year 2012 and is supported by the Dudani Group of Companies with an annual turnover of 600+ crore. It provides an exposure to mining, coal processing and beneficiation and transportation, iron & steel and real estate. Medhavi Foundation is the training partner for National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). In the past 8 years, Medhavi has impacted 1.2 lakh students through their various government sponsored, CSR and paid program initiatives. Medhavi owns and runs 100+ training centres across India including 9 Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) in Contai (West Bengal), Bongaigaon, Hailakandi, Kokrajhar, Silchar (Assam), Aizawl (Mizoram), Singtam (Sikkim), Hazaribagh (Jharkhand), and Jamui (Bihar).

Medhavi Foundation is empanelled with the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship as a Third-Party Aggregator (TPA) and Basic Training Provider for promotion of apprenticeship training under the National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme.
